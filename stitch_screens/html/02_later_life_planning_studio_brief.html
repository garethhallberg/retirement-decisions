Later Life Planning Studio: Concept & Design Brief Core Philosophy: - Lead with life, follow with money. - Health as an enabler of ambition. - Scenarios that resonate personally ("Yes, that's me"). - Focus on spending and living well, not accumulation and worry. - "The goal isn't the biggest number. It's the best chapter." The Seven-Screen Arc: 1. Screen 1 — The Opening: Minimalist, large serif type: "What does a great day look like for you?" 2. Screen 2 — The Joy Conversation: Flowing, beautifully typeset exchange, wide margins, interview style. 3. Screen 3 — The Canvas Moment: A mood board distillation of the conversation with 3-5 evocative cards. 4. Screen 4 — Health & Energy: Brief, optimistic, focused on physical capability and ambition. 5. Screen 5 — The Money Conversation: Calm, warm, and precise. 4-5 exchanges on assets and spending. 6. Screen 7 — Synthesis: Animated transition of cards and numbers coming together. 7. Screen 8 — The Dashboard: Three scenario columns headed by life headlines, with live-editing capabilities. Visual Language: - Style: Warm editorial (Monocle/Kinfolk). - Typography: Serif display (Cormorant Garamond/Freight Text) and refined sans (DM Sans). - Color: Warm paper tones, deep editorial darks, rich amber/gold accents. - Density: Generous negative space, minimal UI chrome.
